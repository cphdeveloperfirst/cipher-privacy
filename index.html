7. Privacy Features

7.1. End-to-End Encrypted Chat: Messages in CIPHER chat are encrypted using XSalsa20-Poly1305. Only you and your recipient can read them. We cannot access message contents.

7.2. CIPHER Vault: Your private notes are encrypted locally using AES-256 with your personal key. Data is stored encrypted on our servers — we cannot decrypt it.

7.3. Encryption keys are derived from your account credentials and never transmitted to our servers.

8. Raids and Social Features

8.2. What other users can see:

8.3. Your real identity, phone number, and personal details are NEVER shown to other users.

8.4. Clan membership provides protection from raids. Inactive clan members (14+ days) may lose some protection.

9A. Account Deletion Process

9A.1. You may request account deletion through the Application settings (Profile → Delete Account).

9A.2. 30-Day Recovery Period: Upon requesting deletion, your account will be frozen for 30 days. During this period:

  • You cannot access your account
  • Your data remains intact but is inaccessible
  • You can recover your account at any time by logging in with your credentials

9A.3. After 30 days: If not recovered, the following actions are taken:

  • Your $CIPH balance is transferred to the community Prize Pool
  • All personal data (phone, email, username) is permanently deleted
  • All transaction history is deleted
  • KYC documents are permanently deleted from our storage
  • Referral relationships are anonymized
Important: Account deletion is irreversible after the 30-day period. Ensure you have withdrawn any $CIPH tokens you wish to keep before requesting deletion.
